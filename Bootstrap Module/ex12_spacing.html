Exercise 12: Spacing Utilities

Exercise 12.1 – Margin & Padding Utilities

Syntax: {property}{sides}-{size}
Property: m = margin, p = padding
Sides: t/b/s/e/x/y (top/bottom/start/end/x-axis/y-axis)
Sizes: 0, 1 (0.25rem), 2 (0.5rem), 3 (1rem), 4 (1.5rem), 5 (3rem), auto
Margin Examples
m-0 (no margin)
m-1 (0.25rem all sides)
m-3 (1rem all sides)
mt-4 (top margin 1.5rem)
mx-5 (horizontal margin 3rem)
Padding Examples
p-0
p-2
p-4
py-5
px-5
pt-3 pb-1 ps-4
m-3       → margin: 1rem on all sides
mt-4      → margin-top: 1.5rem
mx-auto   → margin left & right: auto (centers block element)
p-2       → padding: 0.5rem on all sides
py-5      → padding top & bottom: 3rem
ps-3      → padding-left: 1rem (start)
